Decisions, Decisions, Decisions

Sometimes it's the smallest decisions that can change your life forever. 
- Keri Russell
October 4, 2018

The date above shows the date I started writing this post.... Today's date is the 27th.. of November.. Yes, that's right. It's been more than a month since I've written a blog post. I'm not very proud of that fact, but I'm also not ashamed of it. I'm human, my life gets hectic. A lot has happened between the 4th of October and now. Plus this allows me to show you just how human I am. I make mistakes, I forget things, and I'll be the first to tell you that I have plenty of things to improve upon. But I'm done allow those facts stop me from taking steps towards my goals. 

One of the main goals for this "blog" (aside from possibly getting paid to write one day) is to be more transparent about my life, my goals, my mistakes, and my successes. I'm not one to offer information about myself and what's going on in my life. You have to ask me questions in order to find out what's going on with me. That's not very healthy for growing relationships, but it's me. Of course, I'm working on it, but there are still certain parts of my life that I share with a very few people (like one, maybe two lol). I'm not about to list them, but I will possibly write about them in future post. [Decisions, Decisions, Decision pt. 1B will be written soon :)]
